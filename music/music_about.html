Marco Zammuto is a passionate connoisseur of the upright bass, spanning the realms from classical to jazz. He holds a Master's degree from the Palermo Conservatory, enriched by an exchange program at the Royal Academy of Music (DK). Marco's Master's degree culminated in a significant achievement - the premiere national performance of Jeff Beal's concerto for electric bass and orchestra. He has been an active sideman, shuttling between Italy and Denmark, gracing stages alongside esteemed luminaries like Peter Erskine, Gianluigi Trovesi, Gregg Field, Bobby Watson, Dorado Schmitt, Samson Schmitt, Monica Mancini, Wawau Adler, Daniele Roccato, Adam Rudolph, Marcello Pellitteri, Mark Dresser, Sebastian Gramms, Barre Philips, among others. Having spent formative years in Denmark, Marco now resides in Milano, seamlessly blending his roles as a dedicated musician, creative content producer, and a driving force in music education and software development
