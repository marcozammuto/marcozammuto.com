Why coding?

I'm a orchestral and freelance musician who embarked on a new journey. One year ago, I made a life-changing decision to transition into the world of coding and mix the two worlds. Every single day, I immerse myself in the world of programming, honing my skills and seeking new opportunities in this exciting field.

My Approach to Coding

My approach to coding revolves around a blend of creativity and precision. Similar to composing symphonic music (like I did), I see coding as an art form, a canvas where I can translate ideas into a functional back-end with a user-centric front-end.

I believe in continuous learning and exploration. Every coding challenge is an opportunity to expand my knowledge base and refine my problem-solving skills. I'm deeply passionate about creating clean, efficient, and scalable code that not only meets functional requirements but also resonates with its users.

I love collaborating with people, especially since I landed my first job as full-stack dev.
